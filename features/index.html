Edith AI: Revolutionizing Medical Transcription and Clinic Management

Edith AI is a medical transcription service designed to streamline clinical note-taking for healthcare professionals, bringing clinic software into the 21st century. Our platform allows you to book patients, create report templates, record sessions, and auto-generate reports with ease. Follow up with patients and securely provide their medical information, making the management of medical clinics simpler than ever.

Note Taking

Our proprietary artificial intelligence model reduces the workload of note-taking without the need to hire a scribe. Edith AI listens to conversations and takes notes for you, providing full transcriptions that can be edited as needed. Our model boasts a 96%+ accuracy rate, specifically tailored to the clinical environment.

Report Creation

Leverage custom reports through our report generator. Create report templates effortlessly, which are then integrated into our intelligence layer. This ensures that any session is automatically turned into a report for easy filing.

Patient Management

Maintain a comprehensive record of your patients. View and update patient information, access important details, and tag patients with specific keywords to quickly prepare for your next visit.

Patient Communication

Stay connected with patients to ensure transparency about what was discussed and the next steps. Share reports and transcripts securely with patients and referring doctors through our portal. Utilize email and text reminders to minimize missed appointments.

Clinics at Scale

Whether managing one clinic or one hundred, Edith AI allows you to easily add and manage multiple clinics. Keep data separate while getting an overview of patient load across all clinics to better manage staffing and resources.

Secure Data Management

We adhere to the strictest data privacy and protection standards, being both PIPEDA and HIPAA compliant. All sensitive data is stored locally in your clinic's home country, ensuring a highly secure data environment.
