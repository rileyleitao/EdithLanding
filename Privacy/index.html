Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Including your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Professional Information: Such as your job title, employer's name, and professional certifications.
  • Transcription Data: Audio recordings, text transcriptions, and any other data uploaded or inputted into our platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including login times, pages visited, and features used.
  • Device and Connection Information: IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system.
  • Billing Information: If you choose to subscribe to our services, we may collect payment details such as credit card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To deliver our medical transcription services, including processing audio recordings, generating transcriptions, and providing access to our platform.
  • Improving Services: To analyze usage patterns and feedback to improve the functionality and performance of our platform.
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and send important notices regarding our services.
  • Marketing: With your consent, we may send promotional emails about new features, special offers, or other information we think you may find interesting.

3. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We have implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to fulfill our contractual obligations.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Object to processing of your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where applicable.

7. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised date will be indicated at the top of the page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@example.com.

By using our platform, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
